Understanding the Impact of Ethiopia

Ethiopia is the oldest independent country in Africa, and second largest in terms of population. Its rich history spans back to the 5th century BC, and holds the oldest human remains of over 3 million years old. From the monarch rule of 980 BC to the contemperary era, Ethiopia's diverse culture has influenced the world in cuisine,music, and society.There are many sights to see in Ethiopia, and within this page you will catch a glimpse of what your travels will entail. From museums to geographic features, Ethiopia will not dissappoint. It is growing as a key destination for people who want to experience the place which brought the first cup of coffee, and see the cradle of humanity
