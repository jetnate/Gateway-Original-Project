Information Technology

Tech Companies in Ethiopia have emerged to compete with the leading tech countries in the Continent. The vibrant tech industry has been pushed in the right direction, due to the current Prime Ministers push for tech reforms. Ethiopia has the seventh largest economy in the continent, and hopes to compete with some of its neighbors in Kenya and Nigeria. Below are some of the emerging IT companies in the country.

ICE Addis

Ethiopias first innovation hut and tech company, iceaddis started as a University Sponsored program for tech hungry young adults in the region. Its a collaborative work environment that hosts office space for startups, creative projects an events.

iCogLabs

This company hosts a team of ethiopias top software professionals that primarily focus in artificial intelligence. The company is based in the capital, Addis Ababa and focuses on delivering quality services to customers accross the world. Their projects have been used by the Ethiopian government to map rivers used for Irrigation.

Zala Tech

ZalaTech is a website design, software devolopment, and Hardware Network Instillation company in Ethiopia. They custom devolop software solutions for companies across the continent and world. They also work on search engine optimization to provide rankings that are long lasting.

Gebeya PLC

Gebeya works to provide IT talent to to companies all over Ethiopia. They provide industry-specific training in software engineering and design to match with companies across the region. Their goals to make Africa the next tech hub of the world!
